About Us

Empowering businesses
with a more connected supply chain

Commitment is our attitude.

Founded in 2015, Roots LogistiCare is a fast-growing Warehousing and Supply chain service provider company with all the necessary services for your supply chain management needs.

Roots LogistiCare has responded to the evolution of the supply chain with an entrepreneurial spirit, and emphasis on technology. We help organizations achieve their goals; we uniquely blend our expertise and resources with an intelligent and focused business approach.

We started our journey as a workforce expert, providing workforce services to various MNCs across country & this years of rich experience gives us the calibre to efficiently manage people, manage people with processes and systems to further boost the overall operational performance.

We develop the right operational solution for your business; companies across many industries leverage our solutions to control costs and improve efficiency.

Whether you are a small regional company or a multi-national corporation, the experts here can keep your logistic requirements & projects smooth and save costs.

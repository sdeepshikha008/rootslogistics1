CONTRACT   WAREHOUSING AND DISTRIBUTION   SOLUTIONS

  • Warehousing involves storing and managing inventory, while distribution encompasses order fulfillment, packaging, and shipping to customers. These services are crucial for businesses looking to streamline their supply chain operations and deliver products efficiently to their target markets.
  • Our efficient contract warehousing & distribution solutions provide real-time inventory visibility and user-customized reports that improve accuracy, flow & efficiency while we facilitate the efficient storage, retrieval, and distribution of products to customers or other parts of a supply chain in a timely manner.
  • These services play a crucial role in the logistics and supply chain management of businesses, helping them store inventory, fulfill orders, and optimize their overall operations.

E-COMMERCE FULFILLMENT

  • E-commerce fulfillment is efficiently and accurately receiving, processing, picking, packing, and shipping customer orders for online retailers and e-commerce businesses.
  • It is a critical aspect of e-commerce operations, as it directly impacts customer satisfaction, shipping costs, and overall business efficiency.
  • We serve as valuable partner for E-Commerce, Retail Fulfilment & B2B Fulfillment businesses helping them streamline their supply chain operations, improve order fulfillment efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance customer satisfaction. This partnership allows e-commerce companies to focus on growth and customer service while leaving the intricacies of logistics and fulfillment to us.

IN PLANT LOGISTICS

  • In-plant logistics, also known as internal logistics or intralogistics, refers to the movement of raw materials, components and sub-assemblies within the manufacturing plant of a company for transformation into finished goods. This can be to/from the stocking points, or to/from the production line, for the transformation into the finished goods. In-plant logistics also entails managing the goods when they’re finished and have come out of production.
  • Our overall aim of in-plant logistics solutions is to achieve the utmost customer satisfaction by focusing on the order and product delivery time.
